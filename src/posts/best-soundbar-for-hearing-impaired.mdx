  #### The price and your budget:


  If you are on a limited budget, then you can invest in a renewed option as well. We do not recommend compromising on the brand, though, as that can create problems for you in the future with the sound system's performance. You should get a used option because that works fine too.


  #### The audio capacity and the extensions:


  One important thing is that all speakers can deliver the same volume for larger rooms. Depending on the size of the room and the speakers' placement, you must check the capacity of them, check the size of your room, and check if the speakers suit you.


  #### What's included:


  Some devices come with additional items like remotes, lights, and even batteries. You need to check what is included in the box and whether you need to buy the cables and docks separately or not.
